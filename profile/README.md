The internet created a generation of people who can:

* Write code with AI
* Integrate APIs
* Launch landing pages
* Ship experiments overnight

And yet… most never cross the threshold.

They stay in vibe mode.

They build fragments.

They chase novelty.
They confuse motion with progress.

Vibe is powerful.

## The Founder Threshold

A founder is not someone who "opened a company."

A founder is someone who:

* Designs systems, not features
* Thinks in distribution, not just product
* Builds architecture, not just demos
* Commits beyond motivation cycles

The transition is uncomfortable.

It requires killing your own improvisation habits.

It requires replacing dopamine with discipline.

It requires building things that outlive your mood.

That transition is the quest.
